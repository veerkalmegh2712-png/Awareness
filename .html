Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact across India.

With a base in Chhattisgarh and operations spanning multiple states, we work through a dedicated network of professionals and volunteers to address critical societal issues.

✅ Section 8 Registered
✅ 80G & 12A Certified
✅ CSR-1 Registered
✅ NITI Aayog Listed
✅ ISO 9001:2015
✅ Tax Exempt Donations

What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

Six powerful initiatives — one shared mission of compassion and change.

Food & Welfare

Project SEVA

Providing nutritious meals and essential clothing to underprivileged communities across India. Over 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed so far.

50,000+ beneficiaries served
Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging the educational gap for underprivileged children through digital literacy, life skills training, and school education support in rural areas.

2,000+ children educated
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Collaborating with self-help groups in rural areas to promote financial independence, skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness among women.

900+ women empowered
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Advocating for sustainability through tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns, conservation workshops, and eco-friendly agriculture support.

20,000+ saplings planted
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Dedicated to the welfare of stray and abandoned animals — providing daily food, medical care, rescue operations, and promoting animal rights across India.

50+ animals fed daily
Skill Development

Project VIKAS

Enhancing youth employability through internships in digital marketing, content writing, data operations, finance, research, and social work fields.

30,000+ interns trained

Social Impact

Why Our Work Matters

Every project we run addresses a real gap in society — from hunger and illiteracy to environmental degradation and animal abuse.

🍽️

Fighting Hunger

Thousands of families across India go to bed hungry. Project SEVA directly addresses this by providing hot meals and clothing to those who need it most.

📖

Education for All

Millions of children lack access to quality education. BachpanShala brings digital literacy and school support to rural children, opening doors to better futures.

👩

Women's Independence

Project UDAAN collaborates with self-help groups to break cycles of dependency, enabling women to earn, lead, and live with dignity.

🌍

Greener Planet

With 20,000+ trees planted, Project PRAKRITI builds awareness and action for environmental conservation at the grassroots level.
